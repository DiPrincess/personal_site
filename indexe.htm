Diana Alexandrovna Kachura

Master's student at KubSU, majoring in 01.04.02 "Applied Mathematics and Informatics"

I strive to develop in the fields of IT and artificial intelligence. I participate in competitions, actively engage in student life, and improve my skills in programming, data analysis, and web technologies.

Personal Development

I was born in 2002 in Krasnodar. Since childhood, I showed an interest in art, drew, attended cultural center classes, and graduated from a music school in piano performance.

I graduated from Lyceum No. 61, where there was a special focus on language learning. By the time I finished school, I spoke Russian, English, German, and a bit of Chinese at an amateur level.

From an early age, I was interested in programming and, in particular, artificial intelligence. I participated in various competitions and always tried to be active in school and university life.

Professional Development

In 2020, I enrolled at KubSU in the "Applied Mathematics and Informatics" program. In 2024, I graduated with a bachelor's degree with honors and continued my studies in the master's program, expected to graduate in 2026.

Since 2025, I have been working as a software engineer in the support division at Nexign. In practice, I apply knowledge in programming, data analysis, and IT processes.

Goals and Future Vision

My goal is to develop in the fields of artificial intelligence and programming and to participate in large IT projects. I plan to complete my master's degree and continue my career in innovative technological fields.

I strive for continuous professional growth, participation in significant projects, and applying my knowledge to create new solutions that combine technology and art.
