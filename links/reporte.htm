Information Search Report

Purpose of the study: Search for information related to the topic of the final project.

Within the framework of the research, a thematic search was conducted on the topic of the master's thesis to collect the most relevant and up-to-date materials for use in creating the library of sources and the links section of the master's personal website. The research focuses on the development of a cloud storage system for the educational process, with special attention to the design, implementation, and deployment of cloud data storage systems.

During the analysis, modern approaches to cloud platform organization were studied, evaluating their capabilities in ensuring security, reliability, and scalability, as well as integration with educational and research systems. Particular attention was given to the use of cloud technologies in educational and research activities, including resource sharing, data storage and processing optimization, and improving the efficiency of the learning process. The collected materials made it possible to form a systematic review that can be used to design and develop a personalized cloud storage system tailored to the specific needs of educational institutions and users.

Information search was carried out using the following search engines:

  • Google
  • Yandex
  • Bing
  • Rambler

For each search engine, queries were generated both in Russian and English, corresponding to the key aspects of the research topic.

The largest number of relevant materials on the research topic was found using the Google search engine, especially for English-language queries. This is due to the extensive indexing of scientific sources in English and the high completeness of search results in technical and educational topics.

The Yandex search engine demonstrated lower coverage overall, especially in the English-language segment, but proved useful for working with Russian-language materials. It helped identify sources that may be less visible in global search systems.

Bing produced good results for English keywords, providing a significant amount of relevant information but with fewer Russian-language results. This search engine was effective for analyzing international literature and publications.

Rambler showed very few relevant materials and is overall inferior in search completeness compared to other systems. Therefore, it is the least suitable for conducting a comprehensive thematic search for this study.

Overall, the analysis of search results helped identify the most productive information sources for developing cloud storage in the educational process. The combination of global and local search engines provided a balanced coverage of both English- and Russian-language scientific literature, which is important for a comprehensive study of modern data storage technologies and their implementation in the educational environment.
